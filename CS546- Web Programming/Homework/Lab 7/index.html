My Travel Destinations

Japan

Japan is a place that exceeds the expectations of anyone that visits. As you walk through its bright, busy streets, you find yourself surrounded by many attractions that satisfy the interests of all. Japan surprises you at every corner as you enjoy the variety of food offered at different restaurants or the lively atmosphere of towns like Shibuya or Tokyo. It's more than just a city; it's a rich mix of different cultures, sights, and experiences that capture your attention and invite you to explore more.

  2. Imperial Palace
  3. Shibuya City

Peru

Traveling to Peru is like stepping into a world where history, nature, and culture converge into an unforgettable adventure. Imagine trekking through the breathtaking Andes mountains, with their towering peaks and valleys, leading you to the mystical ruins of Machu Picchu. In Peru, the culture and artwork stand out against the backdrop of ancient Incan walls, offering a taste of local life from my ancestors. Peru promises a journey filled with inspiring moments and a deep connection to the past and present of this enchanting land.

  1. Machu Picchu
  2. Colca Canyon
  3. Chan Chan

Puerto Rico

Traveling to Puerto Rico offers an enchanting experience with its nature, rich history, and vibrant culture. From the moment you step foot on the country, you're greeted with the warm embrace of its tropical climate. Exploring my father's home country means wandering through the wondrous streets of Old San Juan, where centuries-old fortresses stand guard over the coast, and lively plazas are filled with the aromas of local cuisine. Whether you're relaxing on a sun-kissed beach, hiking through the lush El Yunque rainforest, or savoring the flavors of Puerto Rican delicacies (my personal favorite being empanadas), every experience is a celebration of the island's diverse heritage and natural wonders, making for a truly memorable journey.

  2. El Yunque
  3. Mar Chiquita Beach
