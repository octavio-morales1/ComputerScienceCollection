Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Preparing for Japan

    Bring comfortable shoes as you'll be walking a lot, especially in cities like Tokyo and Shibuya. Also, carry a portable charger for your devices, as you'll be taking plenty of photos.

    At the airport when you first land, buy a portable Wi-Fi block, as the cell reception is sub-par at best. Plan your day ahead of time to visit as many locations as you can.

  2. Exploring Peru

    When visiting Machu Picchu, start early to avoid the crowds and bring sunscreen and insect repellent. For hiking in the Andes, bring comfortable shoes as you will be walking a lot.

    Always remember to walk around with a plentiful amount of water, as you will quickly get thirsty. Since there are a lot of food choices, try something new and small to eat wherever you go, as you will most likely lose weight from a lot of walking.

  3. Enjoying Puerto Rico

    Don't forget to bring comfortable shoes, as it is typical to walk long distances. Also, try local delicacies like empanadas to get a taste of the island's rich culinary heritage.

    Never forget to pack water, as it is very easy to get dehydrated in the hot weather. Since there are a lot of food choices, try something new and small to eat wherever you go, as you will most likely lose weight from a lot of walking.
