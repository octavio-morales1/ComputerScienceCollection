Hiking to Machu Picchu

June 20, 2022

The hike through the Andes mountains was challenging but worth it. The sight of Machu Picchu is something I will never forget with my family. The blend of nature and ancient Incan architecture was truly inspiring.

Discovering Old San Juan

July 10, 2021

Walking through the streets of Old San Juan was like stepping back in time. The colorful buildings and cobble streets were very interesting. Tasting empanadas at a local eatery was a delicious end to the day.
